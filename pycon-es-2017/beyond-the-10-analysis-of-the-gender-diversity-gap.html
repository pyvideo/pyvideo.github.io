Beyond the 10%: analysis of the gender-diversity gap

Sun 24 September 2017
By Daniel Izquierdo
  • YouTube

Description

Women represent half of the population. The technological industry claims that around 30% of their work force are women. And that percentage decreases down to 20% when focusing on the tech. teams.

If we analyze open source communities, those hardly reach 10% of the population as this is the case of the OpenStack Foundation or the Linux Kernel.
