Don’t Panic! A Developer’s Guide to Security

Mon 17 July 2023
By Sebastiaan Zeeff
  • YouTube

Description

[EuroPython 2023 — South Hall 2A on 2023-07-20]

https://ep2023.europython.eu/session/dont-panic-a-developers-guide-to-security

As a developer, you play a crucial role in the security of your projects. At the same time, it can be difficult to know if what you’re doing is enough. Luckily, you don’t have to be a security expert to contribute to the security of your projects. Instead, you can use industry standards as a guide for your approach to security.

In this talk, I will introduce you to a framework that is especially accessible to developers, the [OWASP DevSecOps Maturity Model](https://owasp.org/www-project-devsecops-maturity-model/), and help you get started with a systematic approach to improving the security of your projects.
