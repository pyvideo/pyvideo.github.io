Tidypredict with recipes, turn workflow to SQL, spark, duckdb and beyond

Mon 12 August 2024
By Emil Hvitfeldt
  • YouTube

Description

Tidypredict is one of my favorite packages. Being able to turn a fitted model object into an equation is very powerful! However, in tidymodels, we use recipes more and more to do preprocessing. So far, tidypredict didnâ€™t have support for recipes, which severely limited its uses. This talk is about how I fixed that issue. After spending a couple of years thinking about this problem, I finally found a way! Being able to turn a tidymodels workflow into a series of equations for prediction is super powerful. For some uses, being able to turn a model to predict inside SQL, spark or duckdb allows us to handle some problems with more ease.
