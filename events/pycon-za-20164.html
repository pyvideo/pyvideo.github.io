Event: PyCon ZA 2016

PyConZA is the annual gathering of the South African community using and developing the open-source Python programming language. PyConZA is organized by the Python community for the community. We'd like PyConZA to be accessible to as many people as possible and to foster unique solutions to the challenges we face in Africa.
