Event: Kiwi PyCon 2015

Kiwi PyCon is an annual conference aimed at promoting and educating people about the Python programming language.

The New Zealand Python User Group is proud to present the seventh national Python conference in New Zealand.

The conference starts on Friday the 4th with a day mostly consisting of tutorials, followed by the usual format of two streams of talks on Saturday & Sunday the 5th & 6th of September.

This year, the conference takes place at The University of Canterbury which is in the Riccarton suburb near Christchurch CBD.
